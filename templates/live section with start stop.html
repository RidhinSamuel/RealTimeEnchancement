Video Enhancement
Using ESRGAN

ESRGAN (Enhanced Super-Resolution Generative Adversarial Networks) is a state-of-the-art method for image and video super-resolution. It enhances the resolution of images and videos, making them clearer and more detailed. ESRGAN uses deep learning techniques to achieve high-quality results, making it a powerful tool for various applications, including video enhancement.

Photo
Enhancement
Using ESRGAN

ESRGAN uses deep learning to upscale images, improving their resolution and clarity. This method is particularly effective for restoring details in low-resolution photos, making them look sharper and more detailed.

Video
Enhancement
Using ESRGAN

ESRGAN takes each frame of a video and applies deep learning techniques to upscale it while preserving fine details and textures. It enhances resolution by reconstructing high-frequency features, making the video appear sharper and more realistic. To maintain high performance, optimizations like model pruning, tensor acceleration, and efficient GPU utilization are used.
